Testing Arterial Line

Verify arterial cannula position and circuit integrity before initiating cardiopulmonary bypass

1
Closed loop communication
 "Testing arterial line"

2a
Pulsatility check
⚠Warning
For centrifugal pumps, see Circuit Reference →
  • Remove arterial clamp
  • Matches patient pressure

Circuit Reference

  • Centrifugal only → RAP mode on
  • Add Clamp 3 (outlet, centrifugal pump)
  • Remove Clamp 1
  • Remove / open Autoclamp #2

2b
Test Volume
  • Give 100 mls FAST
  • Observe reservoir volume & line pressure ↑ & ↓ to baseline (MAP)

Expected Response

  • Reservoir volume decreases
  • Arterial line pressure rises
  • Pressure returns to patient's MAP
  • No unexpected, sustained pressure elevation

2c
Reclamp Arterial Line
Roller + centrifugal
  • Centrifugal only → apply to Position 1, engage autoclamp, remove Clamp 3
